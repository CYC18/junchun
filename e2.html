About JunChun

Litai Stainless Steel Co., Ltd. was established in the 1970s. At the beginning of its establishment, it was engaged in the business of stainless steel trading, stainless steel material polishing and material processing. Due to the company's professional technology and strict implementation of quality control and good service quality, its performance has continued to grow. 

 

In 1989, he began to engage in more stringent quality stainless steel iron parts for national houses. By 1993, we also undertook the stainless steel works for the Beitou Station of the Taipei MRT Project, and the stainless steel works and system formwork works for the four stations of the Taipei MRT Zhonghe Line. 

 

Because the company continues to increase its business and serve customers with more professional technology. We established Junchun Engineering Co., Ltd. in 1997 to engage in steel structure plant engineering and bridge construction equipment (supporting advanced work vehicle manufacturing, installation, and relocation). Stainless steel engineering design, manufacturing and installation, Taipei MRT Xinzhuang Line 570G standard And 570C standard stainless steel iron parts, etc.
